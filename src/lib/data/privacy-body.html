01 Introduction

This policy applies to the Wefeasto website and to the personal information we handle as its data controller. We follow India’s data-protection framework, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and applicable rules. By using the site, you agree to the practices described here.

If you have any questions as you read, you can reach us any time at privacy@wefeasto.com.

02 Information we collect

We only collect what we need to run the shop and improve it. That falls into a few groups:

Information you give us

  • Account details — your name, email address and password when you register.
  • Order details — delivery address, phone number, and the items in your order.
  • Communications — messages you send us, reviews you post, and newsletter sign-ups.

Information we collect automatically

  • Usage data — pages you visit, products you view and searches you run.
  • Device data — browser type, device and approximate location from your IP address.
  • Cookies & local storage — small pieces of data stored by your browser (see section 4).

Payment information

When you pay, your card or UPI details are collected directly by our payment providers. We receive a confirmation of payment and limited details such as the last few digits of a card, but we do not store your full payment credentials on our own systems.

03 How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • process, pack, deliver and confirm your orders;
  • provide customer support and handle returns or refunds;
  • manage your account and keep it secure;
  • send you service messages, such as order and delivery updates;
  • send marketing — like our monthly recipes and offers — where you have opted in;
  • understand how the site is used so we can improve it; and
  • detect and prevent fraud and meet our legal obligations.

We rely on your consent, our contract with you, our legitimate interest in running the business, and our legal duties as the lawful bases for this, depending on the activity.

04 Cookies & local storage

Cookies and browser local storage let the site remember things between clicks and visits. On Wefeasto:

  • Essential storage keeps your shopping cart. Your cart is saved in your own browser’s local storage (under the key wefeasto_cart) so it is still there when you come back — it stays on your device.
  • Preference cookies remember choices such as recently viewed items.
  • Analytics cookies help us count visits and see which pages are popular, so we can make the site better.

You can clear or block cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Essential storage is needed for the cart and checkout to work, so turning it off may stop you from ordering.

05 How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only where needed to run the shop, with:

  • Delivery partners — couriers who need your address and phone number to deliver your order;
  • Payment providers — to process UPI and card payments securely;
  • Technology providers — hosting, email and analytics services that operate on our behalf under confidentiality obligations; and
  • Authorities — where we are legally required to, or to protect our rights, customers or the public.

If our business is ever reorganised, merged or sold, your information may be transferred as part of that, subject to this policy.

06 Data retention

We keep your personal information only for as long as we need it for the purposes above — for example, to fulfil your order and provide support — and to meet legal, tax and accounting requirements. When we no longer need it, we securely delete it or anonymise it so it can no longer identify you.

07 Your rights & choices

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • access the personal information we hold about you;
  • ask us to correct information that is wrong or incomplete;
  • ask us to delete your information where it is no longer needed;
  • withdraw consent you have given, at any time;
  • nominate someone to exercise your rights on your behalf; and
  • complain to us or to the relevant data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these, email privacy@wefeasto.com. We may need to verify your identity first, and we will respond within the timelines set by law.

08 Marketing communications

If you join the Wefeasto breakfast club, we will send you about one email a month with recipes and offers. You can opt out at any time using the unsubscribe link in any marketing email, or by contacting us. Opting out of marketing does not stop essential service messages about orders you have placed.

09 Data security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures — such as encryption in transit, access controls and trusted service providers — to protect your information against loss, misuse and unauthorised access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please help by keeping your password confidential.

10 Children’s privacy

The site is intended for adults and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental consent. If you believe a child has given us information without consent, please contact us and we will delete it.

11 Third-party links & services

Our site links to marketplace partners such as BigBasket, Amazon and Flipkart, and to our social media pages. These are operated by other companies with their own privacy policies. This policy does not cover them, so we encourage you to read theirs when you visit or buy through them.

12 International data transfers

We are based in India and primarily process your information here. Some of our service providers may store or process data in other countries. Where information is transferred outside India, we take steps to ensure it remains protected in line with applicable law and this policy.

13 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services, technology or the law change. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page, and we will highlight significant changes where appropriate. Please check back from time to time so you stay informed about how we protect your information.
